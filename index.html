Services

SOURCING, RECRUITMENT AND HUNTING

Our job consists in the search and pre-selection of candidates
for our clients according to their necessities.
Some of our tasks include:

Define the Brief and Pitch (of the company and the position).
Post the Job Search in LinkedIn, Job Portals, social networks (if needed).
Search the profile in our Database, LinkedIn and contact candidates.
Present preliminary candidates to the company.

Interview candidates pre-selected by the company.
English and Portuguese assessment (if needed).
Detailed report of the candidates to the company.
Feedback to candidates and follow up of the processes.
